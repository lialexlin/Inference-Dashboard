How to use this page

Wave 1 → Wave 2 rotation playbook

Wave 1 is the primary beneficiary re-rating first as the market prices a demand shock — the names everyone already owns. Wave 2 is the lagging and second-order re-rating: supplier and adjacent industries that follow weeks or months later as consensus traces the value chain.

How to read the wave map: industries with large positive medians have already re-rated — that's wave-1 territory. Industries that are flat or negative despite their customers ripping are the wave-2 hunting ground. Every name is bucketed by what it actually does, so you can see plainly where money is and isn't rotating across the whole large-cap tape. Click a cell to drill into its constituents.

Tracked players (green badge) are the dashboard stocks you already follow — many are US-listed, so they anchor your orientation directly. Click a ticker to open Yahoo Finance, or the tracked badge to jump to its dashboard page.
