How to use this page

Wave 1 → Wave 2 rotation playbook

Wave 1 is driven by tech/demand alpha you can't catch early — the primary beneficiary (e.g., GPU makers) re-rates first as the market prices the demand shock. Wave 2 is the lagging supply-chain re-rating: feeder sectors (substrates, test, packaging, optics) follow weeks or months later as consensus traces the value chain.

How to read the wave map: sectors with large positive medians have already re-rated — that's wave-1 territory. Sectors that are flat or negative despite their customers ripping are the wave-2 hunting ground. Click a sector cell to drill into its constituents in the leaderboard, then click a code to open Yahoo Finance TW for rapid research.

Tracked players (green badge) are the 66 dashboard stocks you already follow. They anchor your orientation — if TSMC is +30% (wave 1) but the substrate/packaging names in the leaderboard are still flat, that's the wave-2 signal. Use the sector filter to isolate the lagging feeder and size the opportunity.
